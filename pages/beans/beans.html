Ingredients

  • 1 cup beans (your choice)
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • Chicken Broth
  • Bay Leaves
  • salt
  • Misc. Arotamics
  • Oil

STEPS:

  1. Clean and rinse beans
  2. Add 3:1 bean ratio of water, add aromatics, and beans
  3. cover with water, add chicken broth, bring to boil and then simmer
  4. Slow cook for about 2 hrs, or until beans tender. (Stir occasionally)
  5. Add salt 30 before removing from heat
  6. Let cool, Serve!
