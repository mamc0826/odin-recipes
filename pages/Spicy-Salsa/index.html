Ingredients

  • 5-6 Tomatoes
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • Chicken Broth
  • 8-12 Serrano/Arbol Chile Peppers
  • 3-5 Habanero Peppers
  • Salt
  • Oil

STEPS:

  1. oil in frying pan
  2. add and cook until slightly black on one side (dont cook too much) chiles, tomatoes, onion, garlic
  3. cover with water, add a spoonful of chicken broth and bring to boil then simmer until tomatoes tender (save salsa broth for later)
  4. once put in blender, add a spoonful of salt and blend.

    **Add salsa broth for more liquidy salsa, less for chunky.

  5. Let cool, Serve!
